📝 CHARTING INSTRUCTIONS & NOTES
"trade what you see not what you think"
  • 📈 Confirm the higher timeframe trend before entering on lower timeframes.
  • 🎯 PAY ATTENTION TO KEY LEVELS.
  • 🔍 Identify key levels (support/resistance, previous highs/lows) on weekly and daily charts.
  • 📐 Fib can only be created on 3 or more daily candles during an uptrend and 4+ daily candles on a sell against the trend.
  • 📊 Trade everything within the 4 x daily fibs and do not be scared to take a few losses.
  • 🛡️ Risk management first: Never risk more than 1% of your account per trade.
  • 📏 If zone is 30 pips or more then split the order position over top and bottom of zone.
  • 🌊 PLAN IN BIG SWING MOVES for news event like BOE & NFP beforehand.
  • 📓 Keep a chart journal: Screenshot setups, note why you entered, review weekly.
These are core rules for disciplined charting. Update as your edge evolves. — SR Vault
